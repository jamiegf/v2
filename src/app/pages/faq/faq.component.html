We are the team behind I-Pools host limited and our brand name is MiPools. Our aim is to design and create fun, safe games for you to play as an alternative to betting!

We offer different ways to play, the majority of the games are pool-based which means the prizes are increased by you, the player! However, watch out for our fixed cash or merchandise prizes. If you want to be notified of new features, exclusive pools and prizes join our newsletter.

Our games are topical across sport and entertainment, and we have been known to add the occasional current affair game. To see what we will be following go to our big event calendar.

If you have any requests for games or like a specific category please do email in your requests and ideas.

Game play and rules

In its simplest form it is a game of skill players pay an entry fee to enter. Your entry fee gets added to a pot. So, when more players pay in the prize increases. We create the games; you create the prize.
We encourage players to tell friends and family to play as not only does it increase the prize, but it is also more fun and challenging when there are more people to play with. It does mean that more players could share the prize, but we see this as a nice problem to have! We want you to win on MiPools!

Fair Play is important to us, and as our community grows, we have plenty of ideas up our sleeves to keep everyone playing and happy.

CRICKET

Pick Five
Make your combined 5 a side from the teams playing in the upcoming match. The better your picks perform, the higher they will score. The player(s) whose picks score the most will be our winner.

FOOTBALL

Pick Four Teams to Score
Pick a team from each pot to score the most goals in the upcoming fixtures. Players will receive a point for every goal their teams score. The player(s) whose picks score the most will be our winner.

Pick Four Players to Score
Pick a player from each pot to score in the upcoming fixtures. Players will receive points for every Goal, Penalty Goal and Assist their players achieve. The player(s) whose picks score the most will be our winner.

Pick Five
Make your combined 5 a side team from the teams playing in the upcoming match. The better your picks perform, the higher they will score. The player(s) whose picks score the most will be our winner.

Jackpot Rollover Coupon
Our biggest prize pool. To win, you must correctly predict a Win or Draw across 10 Matches. If no player scores 10/10, the prize will roll onto the following weekend. There are near miss consolation prizes for entries that score 8/9 out of 10.

Match Rollover
For the selected match, you must correctly predict the number of Goals, Cards and Corners in the match. If you correctly predict all 3 you will be our winner. If no player scores 3/3, the prize will roll onto the next match.

Football Coupons
Predict a Win or Draw across chosen matches. Every correct answer will score you points. The player(s) who scores the most will win or share the prize.

Match Predictors
Make 5 Predictions on the match. You will be asked questions on in game events such as the result, a player to score, the minute of the first goal plus more. Every correct answer will score points, the player(s) who score the most will win or share the prize.

Yes/No Predict 7
Make 7 simple Yes or No predictions for the upcoming match. Every correct answer will score points, the player(s) who score the most will win or share the prize.

Weekly Survivors
Select a team to win their upcoming match. If they win, you will progress to the next round. If they lose or draw, you are eliminated. You can only pick each team once so make your choices carefully. The last remaining player(s) will win or share the prize.

GOLF

Fantasy Four Balls
Pick your Four players for the upcoming tournament. The better your picks perform, the higher they will score. The player(s) whose picks score the most will be our winner.

Tournament Survivor
Select a player to score under par each round. If they do, you will progress to the next round. If they do not, you are eliminated. You can only pick each player once so make your choices carefully. The last remaining player(s) will win or share the prize.

RACING

Horse Racing Rollover
For the selected race, you must correctly predict which horse will finish 1st, 2nd and 3rd. If you correctly predict all 3 you will be our winner. If no player scores 3/3, the prize will roll onto the next race.

Daily Pick 6/7/8
Pick your horses for the upcoming meeting. The better your horses perform at the races, the higher they will score. The player(s) whose picks score the most will be our winner.
